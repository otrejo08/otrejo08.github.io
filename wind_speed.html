Average Wind Speed

No significant correlation was found between latitude and wind speed. Frequency of wind speed, however, can be observed. The most common observation was speeds between 0 and 10 mph. Observations decrease in frequency as you increase wind speed with rare occurrences of wind speeds greater than 30 mph.
